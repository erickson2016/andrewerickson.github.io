SAE Aero Design

Competion Requirements

Design and build an aircraft capable of carrying an internal payload, water bottles, nerf footballs and gliders. The water bottles and footballs are to be droped within a 100ft circle on the ground from an altitude of at least 100ft. Gliders are released at an altitude of 50-100ft at least 200ft from the target. 

Each year the team designs and builds a new aircraft to satify the rule requirements for that year. There is also a detailed design report and design presentation for each compeition that the team must complete alongside the aircraft. This aircraft is then flown in the compeition in Florida which will be March 5-8th this year.
